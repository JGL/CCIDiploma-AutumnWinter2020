Hi!
Welcome to our eleventh experiment/play session!
By the end of this workshop, you'll have done the following things:
  • Experimented with Video and Computer Vision examples in openFrameworks
  • Played with the Image Processing and Computer Vision chapter of the oFBook
For 20 minutes, experiment with the video and computer vision examples that come with openFrameworks. What is the most interesting? Why? What other examples might be useful? Why? What other addons? Where from?
For 40 minutes, play with the Image Processing and Computer Vision chapter of the oFBook. Who is it written by? What are the most interesting examples? Shout me on the CCI Slack if you are stuck.
Thanks!
